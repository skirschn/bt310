About Me

IT Consultant

Starting at the age of 14, I developed a company website and built my own computer. This began my career as an IT pro. I spent 5 years working with Managed IT Services Companies and then founded SJK IT Consulting in August of 2015 to provide services to both residential homes and corporations.

Entrepreneur

After starting my own business, I realized my true path and identity was as an entrepreneur. In May of 2017, I founded MiraSaves Inc. with a friend of mine from Stevens. We truly found a problem with the lack of mental health care technologies and developed a digital therapeutic to help assist with Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) recovery. 

Philanthropist

I have always been active in helping others and fundraising for great causes. Upon entering Stevens Institute of Technology in the Spring of 2016, StevensTHON was founded and I eventually became the Executive Director overseeing StevensTHON to raise $30,000 and becoming the Best New Dance Marathon in the country.
